THE PROBLEM

Roads Are Killing People Silently

Blind curves, zero visibility, and absent warning systems in rural & ghat areas make every drive a gamble.

Zero Visibility

Blind curves and turns with no line-of-sight cause head-on collisions at high speed.

Adverse Weather

Fog, heavy rain, and dust reduce visibility to near zero, making static signboards useless.

No Streetlights

Rural and ghat roads lack adequate lighting infrastructure, making night driving deadly.

Poor Connectivity

Remote areas lack 4G/5G coverage, making cloud-based solutions unreliable.

Static Signboards

Passive reflectors and signs offer no real-time intelligence about approaching vehicles.

High Infrastructure Cost

Existing smart road systems are expensive and impractical for rural deployment at scale.
